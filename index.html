About Me

Hello, I'm Mike! I have recently entered the world of IT and love the possibilities that have opened up before me with the technologies now at my fingertips.

I spent most of 2020 setting up my own blog via GoDaddy/WordPress before becoming more and more curious about how these programs were working. Which led me to start learning HTML/CSS/JavaScript.

By September that year I was convinced that this is what I wanted to do for the rest of my life! And so I enrolled in SAIT's Web Development program, and completed that in Spring of 2021.

Now specializing in Front-End React development, there's nothing I enjoy more than learning and playing with code. Every day challenging myself to create or learn something new, and add it to my growing toolset.
