Where it all began

In May of 2020 I began looking into ways of setting up a small personal blog about gaming and related equipment which I could then turn into a small affiliate-marketing site.

I began to stream, record and upload videos of my gameplay, and blog about the events that transpired in the games I was playing or had played before. That led into a more serious take on streaming and the possibilities that lie with being a twitch affiliate or partner.

But I still wanted to build my website, and I found Godaddy to be far too limiting, so I switched to WordPress and now have a much prettier finished product.

I then taught myself how to manage and update my site, and continue to learn how to improve its performance and SEO.

But I kept stumbling across terms like 'insert HTML' or 'insert custom CSS' and knew there was still more, so I began to teach myself code.

Starting with lessons on W3Schools, and supplemented with practice on coding apps like Mimo. I then chose to enroll in a Web Development course at my local college.
